Built on Grounded Answers

Grounded, not guessed

The system is strictly confined to the retrieved text context. If the information is not in the document, it refuses to answer rather than hallucinating.

Cited, not trusted blindly

Every generated claim is backed by inline citations mapping directly to the exact page and source file, enforcing transparency at scale.

Local-first, not rate-limited

Relying on a local, pure-Python fallback database and sentence-transformer embeddings ensures that vectorization never bottlenecks your workflow.

Built for real documents, not toy demos

Custom sliding-window character chunks respect word boundaries, ensuring critical financial figures and legal clauses remain intact.

01

Parse & Chunk

Incoming PDFs undergo page-by-page extraction. A custom character-based sliding window algorithm splits text precisely on word boundaries to preserve semantic density.

02

Embed & Retrieve

Text segments are immediately vectorized using all-MiniLM and stored locally. User queries undergo the same process to retrieve top-matching document contexts via cosine similarity.

03

Generate & Cite

Retrieved passages are injected into the Gemini context window under strict instruction sets. Responses are returned in natural English, fully annotated with file and page references.
